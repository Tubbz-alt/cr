 1 To update to a new version of Lucene:
 2 
 3 1. Change the version number in the following files:
 4     apps/samples/.classpath
 5     apps/samples/Ensemble8/build.gradle
 6     apps/samples/Ensemble8/legal/lucene.md
 7     apps/samples/Ensemble8/nbproject/project.properties
 8     build.gradle
 9 
10 
11 2. Do a clean build of apps:
12 
13     gradle clean
14     gradle sdk apps
15 
16 NOTE: if upgrading to a new major version there are usually compilation errors that need to be fixed.
17 
18 
19 3. Regenerate the index files:
20 
21 $ cd apps/samples/Ensemble8
22 $ rm -rf src/generated/resources/ensemble/search/index
23 $ ant -Dplatforms.JDK_1.9.home=$JAVA_HOME clean ensemble-generate-search-index jar
24 $ rm src/generated/resources/ensemble/search/index/write.lock
25 $ git add --all src/generated/resources/ensemble/search/index
26 
27 
28 4. Test it by running Ensemble and entering text into the search box (upper right)
